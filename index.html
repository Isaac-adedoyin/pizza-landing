Artisanal Electric Deck Oven

At the heart of Pizza Colombia lies our state-of-the-art electric deck oven, baking at a blistering 450°C (840°F). This advanced, precise heat technology distributes temperature perfectly across the stone deck, producing our signature leopard-spotted crust and airy Neapolitan crumb in under 90 seconds.

Fresh & Premium Ingredients

A great pizza starts with perfect basics. We import genuine San Marzano tomatoes grown in the volcanic soil of Mount Vesuvius for our sauce, topped with fresh buffalo mozzarella, aromatic local basil, and a generous drizzle of premium extra virgin olive oil.

Neapolitan Meets New York

Can't decide between a soft, pillowy Neapolitan pie and a crispy, foldable New York slice? We master both. Enjoy the delicate tenderness of our electric-baked artisan sourdough Neapolitan crust or the satisfying, savory bite of our thin-crust New York style pizzas.
